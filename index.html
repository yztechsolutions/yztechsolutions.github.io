FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

Common repairs like screen and battery replacement are usually completed within 1-2 hours. More complex repairs like motherboard issues may take 24-48 hours depending on severity. We'll provide an accurate time estimate after diagnosis.

We use high-quality replacement parts that meet or exceed original equipment manufacturer standards. All our parts come with warranty. We believe in transparency, so we'll always inform you of the quality of parts being used before any repair.

We provide warranty on all repairs. Screen replacement has 6 months warranty. Battery replacement has 3 months warranty. Motherboard repair has 3 months warranty. Water damage repair has 30 days warranty. Warranty covers parts defects and workmanship issues.

Yes! We take data privacy very seriously. During most repairs, your personal data remains intact. For motherboard repairs, we take extra care to protect your data. We will never access or share your personal information.

We repair all major brands including Apple iPhone, Samsung Galaxy, Google Pixel, Huawei, Oppo, Vivo, Xiaomi, OnePlus, and more. If you have a specific device, feel free to contact us to confirm.

We provide on-site repair service. We recommend calling ahead to ensure fast response. You can call 0451 879 888 or 0447 603 233 to schedule an appointment.
